Our Story

Senco Golds & Diamonds — A Legacy of Trust

For over four decades, Senco has been synonymous with trust, quality, and exquisite craftsmanship. What began as a small family workshop in Kolkata has blossomed into one of India's most beloved jewelry destinations.

Every piece of Senco jewelry tells a story — of tradition, of celebration, of love. Our master artisans combine time-honored techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that transcend generations.
